Total Emission in the world

The map indicates the total emissions in different country in 2022. And it uses the depth of the color to indicate the size of the emissions, and the darker the color, the larger the emissions

Total emission in the specific continent

This chart shows different continents having different total emission for different fields. And use the color to identify different continents. In addition, the size implies the emission amount. The bigger the size, the more emissions. Text annotations are used for showing the continents with high emissions.
