Total Emissions

This page shows analysis of total emissions in 2020

Total Emission in the world

The bar chart shows that the elemnet having the most emission is 'Emssion (CO2eq) (AR5). And we can find that Asia having the most emission regardless of elements of gas. Then, the Americas having the second emission in the world regardless of element. Therefore, Countries in Americas and Asia need to Pay attention to the strict control of 'Emssion (CO2eq) (AR5)'' standards.

Total Emission Elements

The map indicates the total emissions in different country in 2022. And it uses the depth of the color to indicate the size of the emissions, and the darker the color, the larger the emissions. We can find china has the darkest color, thus we can state china has hihest total emission and it will indluence the ecological environment. In addition, we can find united states of America and India also has dark color but not as deep as China. Thus, these two countries need to notice the emission of hameful gas.

Total emission in different fields

This chart shows different continents having different total emission for different fields. And use the color to identify different continents. In addition, the size implies the emission amount. The bigger the size, the more emissions. Text annotations are used for showing the continents with high emissions. And by looking the chart, we can find most of the emissions are concentrated in Asia. The continent with the highest emissions is also Asia and America. Thus, it may lead the greenhouse effect, the pollution of the atmosphere, and then affect the growth of crops and normal human activities, and even affect the normal breathing and health of people. In addition, we can find many emissions in the filed of agri-food system, thus many countries should notice emission of harmful gas in this field in the future.
